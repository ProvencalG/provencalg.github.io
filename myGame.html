About the project

A narrative experience

An immersive narrative experience where everything is centered around a phone. As a police officer, you answer calls and -maybe- resolve problems.

Entirely made with the Unity Engine and aiming for a Steam release for fall 2022.

From a student project

It started with a student project at ISART Digital Paris with a two game designers team:

Robin Poirier and myself, Eddy Briand.

As crude as the project was, people had fun with the prototype. That's why I continued the development, with Robin still helping with the design and writing.

Thought for a phone

You can play with the keyboard, a classic controller, or the phone controller which I created.

All your inputs are made with the phone: composing a number, picking up, hanging down, picking answers, launching interventions, navigating menus... You can even hear the callers from the phone!
