ISART Digital Paris

One of the best Game dev school in France. I was able to learn core and level design, UX, gamification, Unity and many other skills during many student projects and exercises.

Stealth game, Run & Gun, One button game, Makey Makey game and controller, Super Mario Bros levels, Game Jams, UX redesign, Narrative design and rational Level design challenges, Unity projects... The school's structure guided my learning of design and software production.

CS50x by Harvard

An online program (MOOC) divided into 11 weeks which allows students from around the world to take the CS50: Computer Science Fundamentals course by the Harvard University.

Thanks to amazing classes, challenging exercises and great evaluating tools, I was able to learn computer science the best way possible.

C, Python, HTML CSS Javascript, SQL.
